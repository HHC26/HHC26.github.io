After Hurricane

发表于2022-11-07|更新于2022-11-07|外语
|字数总计:453|阅读时长:1分钟|阅读量:

Welcome to the English Corner.

After Hurricane struck Florida, a lot of debris残骸 remained on roadsides. These places collected water, and mosquitoes [məsˈki:təuz] 蚊子 laid their eggs there.
Soggy潮湿 areas with standing water provide a perfect breeding孵化 ground for mosquitoes to lay their eggs. In places with moving water, fish and other animals eat the baby mosquitoes.
Officers work continuously to control the situation. They do surveillance [sɜːˈveɪləns] 监视, trapping 诱捕, and other analysis, and then they treat处理 the mosquitoes on the ground and from the air. However, days of rain changed the landscape 形势, and the insects have new places to breed. Officers need to monitor the new places and deal with them.
The hurricane also contaminated [kənˈtæmɪneɪt] 污染 waterways, and the excess 额外的water led to mold 发霉in low-lying areas, especially inside structures结构. People are in the middle of an enormous [ɪˈnɔːməs] 极大的cleanup, and officers encourage鼓励 them to try to set the garbage垃圾场 so it won’t collect water.

翻译

飓风袭击佛罗里达州后，大量碎片残骸 留在路边。这些地方收集水和蚊子蚊子 他们在那里产卵。
湿漉漉的潮湿有积水的地区提供了完美的繁殖环境孵化 蚊子产卵的地面。在有流动水的地方，鱼和其他动物会吃蚊子宝宝。
官员们不断努力控制局势。他们进行监视监视, 俘获诱捕, 和其他分析，然后他们治疗处理 地面和空气中的蚊子。然而，连日降雨改变了景观形势, 昆虫有了新的繁殖地。警察需要监控新的地点并处理它们。
飓风还污染了污染水道，以及多余的额外的水导致霉菌发霉低洼地区，尤其是建筑物内部结构. 人们正处于巨大的极大的清理，官员鼓励鼓励 他们试图去倒垃圾垃圾场 所以它不会积水。
